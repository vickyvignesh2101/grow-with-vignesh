The Chai Shop & Co CRM Application

A Salesforce CRM application designed to streamline customer management, sales tracking and business workflow through a modern dashboard.

About Project

The Chai Shop & Co CRM Application was developed using Salesforce CRM to improve customer relationship management and automate daily business operations. The system helps manage customer records, sales process, reports and business workflows through a user-friendly interface.

👥 Customer Management

📊 Reports & Dashboard
⚡ Workflow Automation
📈 Sales Monitoring
🔒 Secure CRM Data
